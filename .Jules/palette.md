## 2026-01-30 - [Password Toggle Micro-UX]
**Learning:** Adding a password visibility toggle is a high-impact, low-effort micro-UX improvement that significantly helps users, especially on mobile, while also providing an opportunity to enhance accessibility with ARIA labels.
**Action:** Always consider adding password toggles to login/registration forms and ensure the icons are correctly loaded by placing the CDN link in the `<head>`.
